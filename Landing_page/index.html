Yes — Routiq is completely free to download and use with no hidden fees. All core features including habit tracking, streak system, focus mode, and avatar evolution are available at no cost. We may introduce optional premium perks in the future, but the full experience will always have a free tier.

Routiq is currently in active development and the App Store and Google Play listings are coming soon. In the meantime, you can download the latest APK builds directly from our Releases page. We'll announce the store launch across our social channels — follow us so you don't miss it!

Absolutely. Routiq is built offline-first — all your habits, streaks, and progress are stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage. You don't need an internet connection to check off habits, run focus sessions, or level up your avatar. Internet is only needed if you opt into cloud sync features in the future.

Your avatar evolves based on your habit streaks. Every 5–7 consecutive days of completing your habits earns you a new level and unlocks a rarer companion character — from Novice all the way to Legendary. There are over 10 unique stages to discover. Consistency is the only currency that matters.

Yes! Focus Mode supports custom session lengths ranging from 1 minute to 2 hours. You can also set a default focus duration for each individual habit during habit creation, so your timer is always pre-configured for the task at hand.

100%. Routiq requires no account or sign-up. All your data lives entirely on your device and is never transmitted to any server. We don't collect analytics, track usage, or sell data. Your habits, your privacy — full stop.
