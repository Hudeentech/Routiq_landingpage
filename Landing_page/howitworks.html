About Routiq

Routiq isn't just a habit tracker; it's a completely gamified, offline-first personal progression system.

Designed to transform mundane daily routines into engaging, visually rewarding quests driven by psychological momentum and streak reinforcements.

Here is everything you need to know about navigating and mastering Routiq.

🎯 The Core Philosophy

"Identity Over Outcomes"

Routiq is built upon the ideology that true change happens when you shift your identity, not just when you hit arbitrary goals. The app visualizes this concept using "Evolving Avatars" and "Identity Statements."

By focusing on becoming 1% better every day (your Consistency score) rather than just checking boxes, you build an unstoppable psychological foundation.

🧭 Step-by-Step Guide

Navigating & Mastering Routiq

1

The Onboarding (Defining Who You Are)

When you first open Routiq, you aren't just asked what habit you want to track. You are asked who you want to become. You set your name and define a core Identity Statement (e.g., "I am an athlete" or "I am undeniably disciplined").

Action:

Choose 2-3 starting habits from the curated presets provided during the intro setup, or skip and define your own completely custom routines later.

Customizing Your Avatar

Your profile isn't a static image—it's a reflection of your state in the journey.

Action:

Head to the Profile tab. Tap on your avatar image to easily swap it across an array of aesthetic choices reflecting your current mood.
Note: You also have a unique side-kick "Companion Mascot" that automatically evolves as you level up!

Avatar Customization Screenshot

3

Managing Habits (The Dashboard)

The Home tab acts as your cockpit. It renders only the habits scheduled for today.

Swiping:

Swiping a habit card to the right marks it as completed and fires off rewarding haptic feedback and animations.

Deep Insights:

Tap directly on a habit card to view detailed historical analytics, consistency insights, and the complete streak calendar.

Dashboard Screenshot

4

Adding New Routines

Growth requires adding new challenges over time.

Action:

Press the main + FAB (Floating Action Button) or go to the Routines tab. Configure color categorization, select a custom icon, and set specific frequencies.

Add Habit Screenshot

5

Leveling Up & Earning XP

Every time you complete a habit, you gain Experience Points (XP).

Master Titles:

Check your Profile to view your Level and Progress Bar. As you hit milestones (Level 5, 10, 20), your title evolves from "Beginner" to "Master" to "Visionary" and your Mascot permanently evolves!

Leveling System Screenshot

6

Focus Mode (Pomodoro Integration)

For habits requiring prolonged concentration, clicking off a box isn't enough.

Focus Power:

Go to the Focus tab. Dial in a timer using the Pomodoro 25/5 technique or any custom duration. Completing a focused session grants Bonus XP!

Focus Mode Screenshot
7
7

Protecting Your Data (Cloud Sync)

Routiq is heavily privacy-focused and built "offline-first". Your data lives on your device, giving you unmatched speed and privacy.

Backup:

Go to Settings > Data & Privacy > Backup Data to securely snap a copy of your progress to the cloud linked to your email.

Restore:

Got a new phone? Log in and hit Import Data in Settings to instantly rebuild your journey.

Community & Feedback

We believe in building with our users, not just for them. If you run into a bug, or have a brilliant idea for a new feature:

  1. Navigate to Settings > Support > Send Feedback.
  2. Choose whether you've found a "Bug" or have a "Suggestion".

Note: This goes straight into our secure Supabase database exactly where developers actively triage feature requests!
